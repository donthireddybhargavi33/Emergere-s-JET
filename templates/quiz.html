Quiz App Instructions

  1. Choose the number of questions you want to answer.
  2. Select the category and difficulty level.
  3. Click "Start Quiz" to begin the game.
  4. Answer each question within the time limit.
  5. Click on your selected answer to choose it.
  6. Click "Submit" to check your answer.
  7. If correct, you will proceed to the next question.
  8. Your score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
  9. You can restart the quiz anytime.
