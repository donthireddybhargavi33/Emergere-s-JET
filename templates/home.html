About JET

JET is your all-in-one web application designed to simplify the assignment, tracking, and reporting of your learning goals.


Engage with your learning through fun challenges - gamification!

Key Features

Seamless Tracking

Monitor your progress in real-time and ensure you’re on the path to success.

Email Automation

Stay informed with timely updates and reminders delivered directly to your inbox.

Automated Reporting

Receive insightful reports that highlight your achievements and areas for growth.

Gamification

Engage with your learning through fun challenges and rewards that keep you motivated.

User Management
Effortlessly add, remove, and manage users with just a few clicks. Our intuitive interface ensures smooth navigation and easy control over user access and permissions.

User Dashboard
Get a bird’s-eye view of all your learning activities and performance metrics. The User Dashboard is your personalized command center, presenting insights and progress in a sleek, digestible format.

Email System
Never miss a beat with our robust email system. Receive automated updates, reminders, and notifications that keep you informed and on track with your learning goals. Stay connected and engaged seamlessly!

Gamification
Level up your learning journey with our gamification features! Earn points and badges for completing challenges and achieving milestones, make yourself engaged and feel yourself motivated through our games which are designed on aspects of upskilling logical thinking and problem-solving skills.
